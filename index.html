Shaheel Singh Attorneys Incorporated is a dynamic, client-focused law firm with offices in Pietermaritzburg and Sandton, Johannesburg. Litigation

Our litigation team has extensive experience representing clients across all court levels — from Magistrates' Courts to the High Court. We approach each matter with rigorous preparation, strategic thinking, and a commitment to achieving the best possible outcome.

We handle both plaintiff and defendant briefs, ensuring our clients receive skilled, responsive representation throughout every stage of proceedings.

  • High Court and Magistrates' Court representation
  • Contractual and delictual disputes
  • Interdict applications and urgent relief
  • Debt enforcement and default judgments
  • Appeals and reviews
Consult With Us

Personal Injury & RAF Claims

Being injured in a road accident or as a result of police action is a traumatic experience. Our personal injury team guides clients through the complex claims process, working tirelessly to secure the full and fair compensation they deserve.

We handle RAF claims on a contingency fee basis — meaning no upfront costs to you. We also represent victims of police brutality and state negligence.

  • Road Accident Fund (RAF) claims
  • General damages — pain and suffering
  • Loss of earnings and earning capacity
  • Future medical expenses
  • Police brutality and state negligence claims
  • Claims on behalf of dependants
Consult With Us

Criminal Law

Facing criminal charges is one of the most stressful experiences a person can go through. Our criminal law team provides clear, strategic legal representation, ensuring that your rights are protected at every stage of the criminal process.

  • Bail applications
  • Defence representation in criminal trials

Labour Law

South Africa's labour landscape is complex and ever-evolving. We advise and represent both employers and employees across all aspects of labour law, from day-to-day workplace issues to complex litigation.

  • Unfair dismissal and unfair labour practice claims
  • CCMA conciliation and arbitration
  • Labour Court and Labour Appeal Court
  • Employment contracts and HR policies
  • Retrenchments and restructuring
  • Disciplinary procedures and grievances

Debt Collection

Unpaid debts can severely impact your financial health and business operations. Our debt recovery team combines legal expertise with strategic negotiation to recover outstanding amounts efficiently and effectively.

  • Commercial and personal debt collection
  • Letters of demand and pre-litigation recovery
  • Summons and default judgments
  • Warrants of execution and asset attachment
  • Garnishee orders
  • Insolvency-related recovery
