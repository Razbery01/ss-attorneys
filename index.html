Litigation

Our litigation team has extensive experience representing clients across all court levels — from Magistrates' Courts to the High Court. We approach each matter with rigorous preparation, strategic thinking, and a commitment to achieving the best possible outcome.

We handle both plaintiff and defendant briefs, ensuring our clients receive skilled, responsive representation throughout every stage of proceedings.

  • High Court and Magistrates' Court representation
  • Contractual and delictual disputes
  • Interdict applications and urgent relief
  • Debt enforcement and default judgments
  • Appeals and reviews

Personal Injury & RAF Claims

Being injured in a road accident or as a result of police action is a traumatic experience. Our personal injury team guides clients through the complex claims process, working tirelessly to secure the full and fair compensation they deserve.

We handle RAF claims on a contingency fee basis — meaning no upfront costs to you. We also represent victims of police brutality and state negligence.

  • Road Accident Fund (RAF) claims
  • General damages — pain and suffering
  • Loss of earnings and earning capacity
  • Future medical expenses
  • Police brutality and state negligence claims
  • Claims on behalf of dependants
